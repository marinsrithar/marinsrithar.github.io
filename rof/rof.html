    Ring of Fire Rules
    • Each player takes turns drawing a card from the deck.
    • Each card has a specific rule associated with it.
    • Follow the rules of the drawn card (e.g., 2 = You, 3 = Me).
    • When a King is drawn, the player creates a new rule.
    • Ace = Waterfall (everyone drinks until the player stops).
    • 2 = You: The player who drew the card chooses someone to drink.
    • 3 = Me: The player who drew the card drinks.
    • 4 = Floor: Everyone must touch the floor; the last to do so drinks.
    • 5 = Guys: All guys drink.
    • 6 = Chicks: All girls drink.
    • 7 = Heaven: Everyone points to the sky; the last to do so drinks.
    • 8 = Mate: The player chooses a mate who must drink whenever they drink.
    • 9 = Bust a Rhyme: The player says a word, and players go around rhyming with it; the first who fails drinks.
    • 10 = Categories: The player names a category (e.g., types of beer), and players must name something in that category; the first to fail drinks.
    • Jack = Never Have I Ever: The player states something they've never done; anyone who has done it drinks.
    • Queen = Question Master: The player becomes the Question Master; if anyone answers their questions, they must drink.
    • King = Create a Rule: The player can create a new rule that must be followed for the rest of the game.

    Have fun and drink responsibly!
